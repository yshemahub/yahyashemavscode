About

Muco is a card game that was designed to help bring loved ones together and, at the same time, help advocate for endangered species.

The Silverback Edition places primary focus on the endangered Silverback Gorillas in Rwanda, with integrated Kinyarwanda language elements.

  • RoleCreative Director, Game Designer, Visual Designer

  • Recognition

Game Summary

Muco is a party game full of plot twists, drama, and some healthy rivalry.

Each player is given an animal family (Muco) and playing cards. The first player to get 5 real Mucos in their family wins it all!

Game Design

Game-Mechanic Design

The objective was to craft a game that brings people together, celebrates cultural diversity, and champions wildlife conservation.

Mechanics were selected through playtesting for progressive difficulty, surprise and delight, achievable goals, and sensory engagement.

Prototype

Game testers were provided with Google surveys for anonymous feedback collection, followed by iterative refinement and further rounds of testing.

Game Design

Muco — Silverback Edition

A party game full of plot twists, drama, and some healthy rivalry.

Image or video — assets/projects/muco/01-hero.jpg (or .mp4)

About

Muco is a party game designed to bring people together while raising awareness for endangered species. The Silverback Edition spotlights Rwanda's endangered mountain gorillas and weaves in Kinyarwanda language elements throughout.

  • RoleCreative Director, Game Designer, Visual Designer
  • ToolsAdd tools used
  • Recognition

Game Summary

Players receive animal families and cards, using playing cards to grow and protect their collections. The first player to collect five authentic Muco cards wins.

Game Design

Game-Mechanic Design

The design philosophy centered on community, cultural celebration, and wildlife conservation. Mechanics were selected through playtesting for progressive difficulty, surprise elements, achievable goals, and sensory engagement.
